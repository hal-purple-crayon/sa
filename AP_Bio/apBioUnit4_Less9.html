        • Big Ideas & Unit Learning Objectives
        • Unit 4 Lesson 1 & 2

          Lesson Overview: Scholars continue to improve their CER/FRQ strategies at an AP Level. Bioluminescence is used to introduce and investigate an example of energy conversion as it relates to the survival and evolution of the Fireflies. This takes scholars in a deep dive of metabolic pathways and strategies.

        • Unit 4 Lesson 3

          Lesson Overview: Scholars will continue to enhance their skills in CER. Data Nuggets are a great way for scholars to practice this process that will occur in the six AP Biology FRQs. Lesson will also cycle back to Unit 3, Enzyme FRQ.

        • Unit 4 Lesson 4

          Lesson Overview: Scholars will explore the symptoms and deaths of seven people, through a university-level case study. This case study will link the metabolic pathway of cellular respiration with the cause of death. This particular case study will spiral their understanding of cellular organelles and push them to do some initial research on parts of the cellular respiration metabolic pathway.

        • Unit 4 Lesson 5–8

          Lesson Overview: This lesson will be a short introduction to Cellular Respiration and pre-lab work for the multi-day investigation that scholars will be completing.

        • Unit 4 Lesson 9

          Lesson Overview: This lesson will provide scholars with a quick review of cellular respiration. Scholars will then take on a biointeractive study that deep dives into the evolutionary history of birds and focuses on size and metabolic rates.

          • Day 9
        • Unit 4 Lesson 10

          Lesson Overview: Scholars will review and discuss their findings to the previous lesson’s question. “Were dinosaurs endo or ectotherms?” The lesson then takes scholars through an introduction to photosynthesis.

        • Unit 4 Lesson 11

          Lesson Overview: Scholars discuss the calvin cycle and compare the processes of cellular respiration and photosynthesis, leading to a quick exploration of modified ways plants complete photosynthesis in extreme environmental conditions.

        • Unit 4 Lesson #12

          Lesson Overview: Scholars will work to complete Unit 4 FRQ #1. This will allow scholars to practice their current understanding of metabolic pathways and continue to enhance their CER strategies.

        • Unit 4 Lesson 13

          Lesson Overview: In this lesson, scholars learn how to use the floating leaf disk method to measure the rate of net photosynthesis (i.e., the rate of photosynthesis minus the rate of cellular respiration).

        • Unit 4 Lesson 14

          Lesson Overview: Scholars will take a brief dive into ecology fundamentals. This lesson focuses on nutrient and energy dynamics through an in-class lab investigation. This is equivalent to completing the four-week-long lab from the college board lab investigations.

        • Unit 4 Lesson 15

          Lesson Overview: Scholars look at the effects of DDT on bird populations and how biomagnification works throughout food chains.

        • Unit 4 Lesson 16

          Lesson Overview: Scholars will take a quiz on ecological topics covered thus far in the course. They will do a deep dive into population ecology, with integrated discussion questions and problems.

        • Unit 4 Lesson 17

          Lesson Overview: Scholars work to practice exponential growth practice problems. They will then work to estimate population size through an investigation.

        • Unit 4 Lesson 18

          Lesson Overview: Scholars dive deep into solving a real world example of population ecology. What factors could be contributing to the decline in the sea otter population? Scholars begin to recognize fields of study and jobs that are available within the life sciences.

        • Unit 4 Lesson 19

          Lesson Overview: Scholars will take a deep look at the chapters they have covered in Your Inner Fish. Scholars will then explore how the predator vs. prey relationships affect the ecosystem and respond to each other.

        • Unit 4 Lesson 20

          Lesson Overview: Scholars continue to work on their CER strategies in the completion of Unit 4: FRQ #2. This FRQ specifically focuses on a spiral review of implementation of scientific investigations, data analysis, and connecting content from Ecology.

        • Unit 4 Lesson 21

          Lesson Overview: This lesson is spiral review time to connect all the content learned in Unit 4: Cellular Energetics.

          Lesson Overview: Unit 4 Test and Unit 5 Materials Launch
